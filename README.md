**Nexill POS is a practical Windows retail checkout and store-management application built for small stores.**

Designed for PC-based counter workflows, Nexill POS helps retail teams manage checkout, products, receipts, invoicing, reports, users, settings, and daily store operations from one clear offline workspace.

It is built for the pace of small retail: keeping checkout fast, keeping product handling organized, and giving owners and store teams stronger day-to-day control. Nexill POS also works smoothly alongside external payment terminals, making it a strong fit for stores that want a reliable retail system without changing the way payments are already handled.

---

## ✅ What you get
* **Fast checkout workflow** built for day-to-day cashier use
* **Offline-first operation** for dependable selling at the counter
* **Product and inventory management** in one practical system
* **Receipts, invoicing, and reporting** for daily store follow-up
* **User roles and store controls** for protected access and cleaner staff management
* **Windows desktop workflow** designed for fixed store counters and retail workstations

## 💻 System Requirements
* **OS:** Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
* **Hardware:** Standard PC, laptop, or Windows-based POS terminal
* **Optional:** Receipt printer and other compatible retail peripherals
